Key Insights

  • College graduates consistently experience lower unemployment rates compared to those without degrees.
  • STEM fields generally show higher starting salaries and better long-term growth.
  • Advanced degrees (Master's, PhD) show significant salary premiums in most fields.
  • Economic recessions impact college graduates less severely than non-graduates.
  • Gender and racial gaps persist in both employment rates and salaries across most fields.
